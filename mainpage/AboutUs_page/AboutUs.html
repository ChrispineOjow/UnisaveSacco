Our Inspiring Journey & History üöÄ

Founded in 2025 by a group of visionary university alumni, UniSave Sacco was established with the core belief that every student deserves access to reliable and empowering financial services. Starting as a small cooperative, we have grown steadily, fueled by our commitment to member welfare and sustainable financial practices. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, adapting to the evolving needs of the student community while staying true to our cooperative roots.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of students achieve their academic and personal financial goals, from funding tuition to starting small businesses. Our history is a testament to the power of collective effort and shared prosperity.
